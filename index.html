Guide

Buying crypto in Russia

Legal status

Russia's 'Digital Financial Assets' law (2020) recognizes crypto as property but prohibits using it for payments. Trading and holding are legal. Mining was legalized with tax requirements. The regulatory framework continues to evolve.

Sanctions impact

Many global exchanges have restricted or exited the Russian market due to international sanctions. Available options are primarily P2P platforms and exchanges that continue to serve Russian users. Always check the current status of platform availability.

Ruble on-ramps

P2P trading with ruble payments via bank transfer (Sberbank, Tinkoff) remains the most common on-ramp. Some platforms support SBP (Faster Payments System) for instant ruble deposits.
